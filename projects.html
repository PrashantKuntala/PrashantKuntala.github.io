Research Projects

Yeast Epigenome Project (YEP) A comprehensive DNA-centric structural view of how proteins are organized into complexes across the genome, including the architectural basis of fundamental gene classes. PCRP Validation A community resource of renewable, high-quality protein capture reagents to understand the critical role the multitude of cellular proteins play in development, health, and disease exo is a collection of command-line utilities for Yeast Epigenome Project and ChIP-exo data analysis and visualization. Locusplotter is a visualization tool for discovering protein binding & co-binding patterns at interested genomic loci or DNA-binding motifs. Stencil is a web templating engine that lets you visualize and share life science datasets.
